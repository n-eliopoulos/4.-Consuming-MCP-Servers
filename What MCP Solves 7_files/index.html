Exercise: MCP vs. Direct Integration

Apply the decision framework to four integration scenarios. For each, recommend MCP or direct integration and justify your choice with a trade-off analysis.

Instructions

  1. Open the sandbox below.
  2. For each of the 4 scenarios, call evaluate_scenario() with the scenario number, your recommendation ("mcp" or "direct"), and your justification.
  3. Your justification should address at least one relevant criterion from the decision framework (composability, discovery, reuse, performance, simplicity).

Scenarios

  1. Single internal API: A Python script that calls one internal REST API to fetch daily trade counts. Used by one team, one application.
  2. Multi-tool agent: An AI assistant for compliance officers that needs access to rule lookups, trade surveillance, market data, and document search.
  3. Compliance checking pipeline: A batch process that checks 10,000 trades per hour against compliance rules. Latency budget: 50ms per trade.
  4. Ad-hoc data exploration: An analyst's AI assistant that connects to whatever data sources are relevant to the current question. Data sources change weekly.
Grading: Recommendation is binary (right or wrong). Justification is checked for mention of relevant trade-off criteria. You need at least 3 of 4 correct to pass.
